A Furniture Colletion

© MUSTAFA AL QURAISHI, CILVIA STUDIOS 2020

This collection is an exploration and challenge of furniture pieces. With a specific focus on the composition of geometries and materials to compliment and juxtapose each other, this collection consists of a stripped back minimalist look akin to a modernist era piece. The collection celebrates the use of metal within furniture, as the a rough anodised aluminium is found throughout each piece. The mechanical stool is a robust and perhaps uncomfortable piece, cold to the touch, with visible bolts, and little cushioning the stool is not created to be comfortable, yet to be a statement piece. The stool like the rest, of the collection boasts a circular form, yet this is juxtaposed with the table, with a contoured surface, the tension line offsets the smooth surface and is then met with the back lip of the table curving up again. With further finishing by a sectored flat front, the table offers a sexy and modern take on the desk table. The composition of table takes a turn from the pure metal aesthetic with the use of mahogany legs, offsetting the cold feel of the collection by bringing a sense of warmth. The dark gloss nature of the mahogany contrasts with the grey trough texture of the aluminium. The legs are further finished with rubber bottoms, akin to the desk lamp which has a rubber bottom piece. Offering a fresh take on the common desk lamp, the aim of the lamp was to have a single touch point where the user can direct light at all angles and directions and to emphasise the mechanical aspect of lamp's function, tying the table and stool together. A Furniture Series is a blend of new materials, forms and styles invoking a cold futuristic look rooted in a simple, warm reality.
