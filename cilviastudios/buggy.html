Aygo Urban Buggy

© MUSTAFA AL QURAISHI, CILVIA STUDIOS 2020

Separable in two parts: the baby seat and its frame, Aygo can be folded within seconds. With one hand, the seat is detached from the frame without having to take the baby out. With the other hand, the frame is collapsed and carried around. The innovative folding system enables parents access to previously inaccessible obstacles in urban areas, such as stairs, and allows parents to navigate through crowded spaces. These include transport facilities, such as the underground tube. Designed primarily for newborn babies, the seat can be carried in one hand while the baby is safely held. The inner shell seat can be adjusted in angle and therefore follows the necessary evolution of the baby’s posture. As for the frame, it consists of a one-hand folding mechanism. Its light weight allows portability but it’s heavy and robust enough to ensure stability along with the angled legs that cover a greater width and span.
