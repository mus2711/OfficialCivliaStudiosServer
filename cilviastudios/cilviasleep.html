Cilvia Sleep

© MUSTAFA AL QURAISHI, CILVIA STUDIOS 2020

With nearly a third of the population suffering from insomnia, and its associated harmful symptoms including: depression, low energy and neurodegenerative diseases. This project has devolved into targeting a solution that will aim to address and aid people suffering from acute or chronic insomnia. By targeting root causes of sleep prevention, the solution will help to neutralise the issues causing loss of sleep such as circadian rhythm disruption via blue light exposure due to late night phone use and utilise aromatherapy and deep sleep audio in order to relax the user and aid in a better nightâ€™s sleep. It simulates relaxing environments by sensory stimulation techniques to induce a better nights sleep. With use of aromas and audio to relax and immerse the user in their favourite places. Transporting the user from their bedroom to their favourite relaxing environments such as the beach. It limits late night phone use, with built in features such as phone lock mode and the purposed placement of the phone on the wireless charging pad inhibits the user from using their phone. It uses red light therapy to induce melatonin production to aid in relaxation, as the light pulsates at a soothing, gentle rate.
